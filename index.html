3DPUG Manchester
A meetup for 3D printing hobbyists in Manchester.

About

3DPUG Manchester was started in June 2012. It is as a meeting for 3D printing enthusiasts to exchange knowledge and advice on building, maintaining and operating hobbyist-type 3D printers.

Our meetings normally consist of a show-and-tell segment, where attendees show what they've been working on over the previous month, then a general discussion about issues and advice.

Our venue also happens to contain a fully-stocked workshop, so 3D printer issues can often be troubleshot and solved on the night!

Meetings

We meet from 7.30pm on the Second Tuesday of every month at Hackspace Manchester.

The exact dates of our upcoming meetings can be found on the HACMan Events Calendar.
